Our initiatives

Six public-interest initiatives built from rural Pakistan for all of Pakistan.

Each initiative starts from a real community need identified through WALI, WANG's rural innovation lab in Balochistan, and grows into a public system that can serve communities across the country.

01

WALI

WANG Lab of Innovation -- Pakistan's first rural innovation lab in Balochistan. Digital literacy camps, youth pathways, AI workshops, and community programs serving children, girls, women, and first-time learners in Lasbela.

Visit WALI
02

Urdu AI

Pakistan's largest Urdu-language AI education platform. 50K+ app downloads, 800K+ learners reached, and a national Dost facilitator network spanning 29+ districts -- making AI accessible in simple Urdu.

Visit Urdu AI
03

PakSpeed

Pakistan's public internet speed test and digital infrastructure research platform. 32K+ users testing connectivity, generating evidence for better internet access and accountability across Pakistan.

Visit PakSpeed
04

PakEducate

Pakistan's first AI-supported bilingual school management system designed around the real operational needs of Pakistani schools, with workflows in Urdu and English.

Visit PakEducate
05

WIRE

Women's Innovation and Rural Enterprise -- a mother-daughter innovation model pairing rural craftsmanship with digital skills, solar-powered production, and market access for women in Balochistan.

Visit WIRE
06

Darwaza

A smart QR-code doorbell app replacing expensive hardware doorbells with a simple, affordable solution. Available on Google Play Store -- making smart access technology available to everyone.

Visit Darwaza
