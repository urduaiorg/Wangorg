## AD GRANT COMPLIANCE REMINDERS

Your grant will be SUSPENDED if:
- CTR drops below 5% for 2 consecutive months
- You have fewer than 2 ad groups per campaign
- You don't have at least 2 sitelink extensions
- Landing pages don't load or aren't relevant to keywords
- You promote commercial products (keep focus on non-profit mission)
- Geographic targeting isn't set
- You use single-word keywords (except branded terms)
- You use overly generic keywords

Your grant stays healthy when:
- CTR is above 5%
- Conversions are being tracked
- Landing pages are fast, mobile-friendly, and relevant
- Negative keyword list is maintained
- Ad copy matches landing page content
- Keywords are specific and intent-driven
