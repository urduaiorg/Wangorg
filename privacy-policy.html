Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Introduction

WANG (We Are New Generation), a registered non-profit organization in Pakistan, is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to wang.org.pk and its associated digital platforms. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website:

  • Automatically collected data through Google Analytics (GA4), including page views, session duration, geographic location (country/city level), device type, browser type, and referral source.
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us via email, such as your name, email address, and message content.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (measurement ID: G-DJ2TGEPJ49) to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. This data helps us improve our website and better serve our community.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Google Ads

WANG participates in the Google Ad Grant program for non-profits. We may use Google Ads conversion tracking (AW-17337315510) to measure the effectiveness of our awareness campaigns. This tracking does not collect personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve our website content, structure, and user experience
  • Understand how visitors find and use our site
  • Respond to inquiries and partnership requests
  • Report on our impact and reach to stakeholders and donors
  • Comply with Google Ad Grant program requirements

Data Sharing

WANG does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with partners and donors for impact reporting purposes.

Cookies

This website uses cookies for Google Analytics and basic site functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information transmitted through our website. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites (urduai.org, walipak.com, pakspeed.com, pakeducate.com, wirepk.com, doorcalling.com). Each site has its own privacy policy, and WANG is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Contact us with any privacy-related concerns

Changes to This Policy

WANG may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
