Impact

Public proof from Pakistan's rural innovation programs.

Aggregate metrics across WANG's six initiatives demonstrate measurable outcomes in AI literacy, digital access, women's empowerment, internet infrastructure, and community learning.

800K+ learners reached through Urdu AI across Pakistan via WhatsApp, YouTube, Facebook, and web

35M+ reach total media reach across platforms

Impact by initiative

Each initiative contributes measurable outcomes to the aggregate picture.

Impact is not just numbers. It is the public documentation, partner validation, and community transformation that makes the work credible and investable.

Urdu AI

800K+ learners across WhatsApp, YouTube, Facebook, and web. 50K+ app downloads. 31 active Dost facilitators across 29 districts. National Urdu-language AI education movement supported by Google.org.

WALI

196+ learners trained through 3-month digital literacy programs. 108 female and 88 male graduates in 2025. 6 AI workshops delivered. Winter digital camp for children. Solar-powered lab serving 40 students daily.

PakSpeed

32K+ users testing internet connectivity. Crowdsourced speed test data generating evidence for better infrastructure and ISP accountability across Pakistan.

WIRE

500+ women empowered, 200+ girls trained. Mother-daughter innovation model pairing rural craftsmanship with digital skills. 60% income increase reported for participating rural women in Balochistan.

Milestones

Impact timeline

2014

WANG’s public journey begins

First website and community storytelling from Ahmed Abad Wang — the start of a 12+ year record (see the Welcome Blog archive).

2021

WALI innovation lab established

WALI (WANG Lab of Innovation) opens in Lasbela to concentrate digital literacy camps, youth pathways, and women’s programs.

2022

Flood response & lab scale-up

Lasbela floods: relief and recovery alongside expanded cohorts; solar-powered lab operations deepen field presence.

2023

Urdu AI launches

Pakistan's first Urdu-language AI education platform launches, quickly growing to hundreds of thousands of learners.

2024

National expansion

Urdu AI Dost facilitator network expands to 29+ districts. PakSpeed crosses 32K users. WIRE empowers 500+ women.

2025

800K+ learners reached

Urdu AI crosses 800K learners and 50K app downloads. WALI graduates 196+ learners. Google.org partnership active.
