# Recommendations from Antigtavity

Based on the **Codex** (Content Enhancement) audit of the live `wang.org.pk` WordPress site, here is the strategic advice for the static revamp.

## 1. Critical "Trust Signals" to Migrate
> [!IMPORTANT]
> The following legal and social proof elements must be present to maintain authority and Ad Grant compliance.

*   **Legal Identity**: Include the NTN (**7426613**) and registration details (Balochistan Charities Act / Voluntary Social Welfare Act) in the footer.
*   **2025 Recognition**: The **K-Electric Karachi Awards 2025** is a major recent win. This should be a featured "Latest News" item on the homepage or added to an "Awards" section.
*   **Institutional Partners**: Preserve and clean up the partner logo strip (FBR, regional commissions).

## 2. Impact Data (The "Numbers that Matter")
The current site has very detailed metrics that should replace any placeholder data in the new static `impact.html` and `index.html`.
*   **Education**: 2,222 Scholarships, 5,335 Re-enrollments, 1,322 Digital Skills trainees.
*   **Reach**: 29M+ AI Education reach, 50M+ Digital Media engagements.
*   **Community**: 50 Homes rebuilt, 875 Climate Leaders, 9 Solar villages.

## 3. Recommended Feature Enhancements (Codex Standard)
*   **Dynamic Impact Counters**: Implement a simple JS counter for the statistics section to make the "Our Impact Unleashed" section feel alive.
*   **Initiative Sub-Branding**: While the main site is "Gold/Blue", preserve the distinct "look and feel" for sub-entities like **WALI** and **Urdu AI** through custom landing page HERO sections.
*   **Video Integration**: The WordPress site features a "Success Stories" video gallery. In the static site, we should use lightweight YouTube facade embeds (lite-youtube-embed) to maintain fast LCP (Largest Contentful Paint).

## 4. Content Gap Analysis
| Existing Site Feature | New Site Action |
|---|---|
| **Hero Slider** (Owl Carousel) | Replace with a high-performance, single-image impact hero with a clear CTA to Urdu AI. |
| **Kaka's Garden** Section | Should be a dedicated section under "Initiatives" or its own landing page if it's a focus area for Ad Grants. |
| **FBR/Charity Logos** | Move from sidebar to a dedicated "Transparency" section in the footer. |

> [!TIP]
> **Priority Recommendation**: The "Urdu AI" CTA should be globally persistent (Header + Footer) as it is the primary revenue engine ($10k Ad Grant funnel).
