## THE REVENUE ENGINE

This isn't just a website. It's a **money machine** for the mission:

```
Google Ad Grant ($10K/month free ads)
    ↓
wang.org.pk landing pages (convert ad clicks)
    ↓
Funnel to urduai.org (monetized with AdSense)
    ↓
AdSense revenue funds operations
    ↓
Also: donor acquisition, partnerships, media coverage
    ↓
Also: organic SEO drives free traffic forever
```

### The Math
- Average Ad Grant account spends $300/month. Top-managed accounts spend $8,650+/month
- WANG currently spends $932/month — 91% wasted
- Target: $9,000+/month spend = 30,000-50,000 clicks/month
- If 20% of clicks flow to urduai.org (AdSense) = 6,000-10,000 monetized visits/month
- At $2-5 RPM = $12-50/month from Ad Grant overflow alone
- Real value: donor acquisition, brand awareness, search rankings

### Key Insight from Research
"Maximize Conversions" bidding (not "Maximize Clicks") removes the $2 CPC cap. This means you can bid on high-value keywords that currently go to competitors. Switch bidding strategy immediately.

## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY: What World-Class Non-Profits Do

Based on research of award-winning non-profit websites (Obama Foundation, WaterAid, charity: water, Farm Africa, SolarAid):

### 6 Principles for WANG

**1. IMPACT-FIRST HERO — Not mission statements**
- Obama Foundation: Bold hero video, immediate mission clarity
- WaterAid: Impact video as hero, emotion before information
- WANG should: Lead with "800,000 learners. 6 initiatives. 1 village." + hero video from ARY News

**2. CONTEXTUAL DONATIONS / ACTIONS**
- charity: water: "$40 gives one person clean water for life"
- GiveDirectly: Shows exact dollar → impact conversion
- WANG should: "Your partnership helps train 40 students per camp" → CTA to contact

**3. INTERACTIVE STORYTELLING**
- Farm Africa: Scroll-based zoom into the word "solution"
- SolarAid: Cursor-following spotlight reveals impact
- WANG should: Scroll-based timeline from 2014 founding → 2026 800K learners (12 years of growth)

**4. VISUAL PROOF STACKING**
- Every claim backed by: photo → video → data → third-party validation → report
- WANG has: ARY News, Express News, Internet Society, AVPN, Google.org

**5. CLEAR SINGLE JOURNEY**
- Best non-profits: 3 clicks or fewer to key action
- WANG: Every page → "Partner With WANG" or "Learn on Urdu AI"

**6. ACCESSIBILITY + SPEED**
- Most visitors from Pakistan (slow connections, mobile-first)
- Static HTML = fastest possible load time
- Cloudflare CDN = served from edge
- Urdu + English bilingual = 2x the audience
