FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Answers to common questions about WANG, its programs, and how to get involved.

What is WANG?

WANG (We Are New Generation) is a registered non-profit in Pakistan. Public roots date to 2014; WALI, the rural innovation lab, opened in 2021. WANG builds digital access, AI literacy, women's innovation, school technology, internet research, and community infrastructure through six initiatives from Balochistan.

What programs does WANG run?

WANG runs six initiatives: WALI (rural innovation lab with digital literacy camps), Urdu AI (Urdu-language AI education with 800K+ learners), PakSpeed (internet speed testing with 32K+ users), PakEducate (school management SaaS), WIRE (women's innovation and rural enterprise), and Darwaza (smart QR doorbell app).

How can I partner with WANG?

Organizations, donors, government bodies, and technology companies can partner with WANG by emailing info@wang.org.pk. WANG welcomes partnerships for digital literacy, AI education, women's empowerment, internet infrastructure research, and school technology.

How can I volunteer with WANG?

Individuals can volunteer as digital literacy trainers, Urdu AI Dost facilitators, content creators, or field support staff. Contact info@wang.org.pk to explore volunteer opportunities.

Is WANG a registered non-profit?

Yes, WANG is a registered non-profit organization in Pakistan under the Societies Registration Act. It holds a Google Ad Grant and operates as a transparent, publicly documented institution.

Where is WANG based?

WANG is based in Ahmed Abad Wang, Bela, District Lasbela, Balochistan, Pakistan 90050. Its innovation lab WALI operates from this location, serving rural communities across the district.

What is the Google Ad Grant?

The Google Ad Grant provides eligible non-profits with up to $10,000/month in free Google Ads. WANG uses its Ad Grant to promote its programs and drive awareness of digital literacy, AI education, and rural innovation opportunities in Pakistan.

Are WANG's programs free?

Yes, all of WANG's core community programs through WALI are free. Urdu AI content is freely accessible. WANG is a non-profit committed to removing barriers to digital access and education.
