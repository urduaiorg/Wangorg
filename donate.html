Support WANG

Back work that already has proof, not just promise.

WANG is a public-interest non-profit with an unusually clear operating model: local delivery through WALI, national reach through products like Urdu AI and PakSpeed, and public proof through measurable outcomes. Support can be financial, strategic, in-kind, or volunteer-led.

Ways to support

Choose the route that matches your capacity.

WANG works best with supporters who care about execution quality, community trust, and long-term utility. This is support for durable systems, not one-week campaigns.

Strategic giving can fund programs, equipment, learning materials, and field delivery in Lasbela.

Ideal for individual donors, small family funds, and people who want to back practical work on the ground.

Institutional partnerships can underwrite training, AI literacy, research, or women’s enterprise programs.

Best for foundations, donor programs, NGOs, public agencies, and corporate social responsibility teams.

In-kind support can remove hard operating constraints: devices, connectivity, curriculum tools, and media support.

Useful for companies and collaborators that want direct operational leverage.

Volunteering expands WANG’s capacity in training, documentation, translation, design, content, and facilitation.

Best for people who want to contribute expertise, not just attention.
