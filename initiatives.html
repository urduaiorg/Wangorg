Initiatives

Six initiatives built from rural Pakistan for all of Pakistan.

Each initiative starts from a real community need identified through WALI and grows into a public system serving communities across the country.

Rural Innovation Lab

WALI

WANG Lab of Innovation -- Pakistan's first rural innovation lab in Balochistan. Digital literacy camps, youth pathways, AI workshops, and community programs serving children, girls, women, and first-time learners in Lasbela. 196+ camp graduates in 2025.

Visit walipak.com
800K+ Learners / 50K+ Downloads

Urdu AI

Pakistan's largest Urdu-language AI education platform. Making artificial intelligence accessible through WhatsApp, YouTube, Facebook, a mobile app, and a national Dost facilitator network spanning 29+ districts across Pakistan.

Visit urduai.org
32K+ Users

PakSpeed

Pakistan's public internet speed test and digital infrastructure research platform. Crowdsourced connectivity data generating evidence for better internet access, ISP accountability, and policy advocacy across Pakistan.

Visit pakspeed.com
School SaaS

PakEducate

Pakistan's first AI-supported bilingual school management system. Affordable SaaS designed around the real operational needs of Pakistani schools with workflows in Urdu and English for student records, attendance, and administration.

Visit pakeducate.com
Women's Innovation

WIRE

Women's Innovation and Rural Enterprise -- a mother-daughter innovation model linking rural craftsmanship, solar-powered production, girls' digital training, and market access for women in Balochistan. 500+ women empowered, 200+ girls trained.

Visit wirepk.com
Smart Access

Darwaza

A smart QR-code doorbell app replacing expensive hardware doorbells with a simple, affordable solution. Available on Google Play Store, making smart home access technology available to everyone in Pakistan.

Visit doorcalling.com
