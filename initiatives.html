Initiatives

WANG is a nonprofit institution first, with flagship platforms and signature programs under it.

WANG is the registered nonprofit that ties everything together. WALI is the rural innovation lab, Urdu AI is the flagship national learning platform, WIRE and ADI are core programs, and other products sit within the wider ecosystem.

Flagship platforms

WANG's flagship platforms — where most of the work happens.

Rural Innovation Lab

WALI

WANG Lab of Innovation -- Pakistan's first rural innovation lab in Balochistan. Digital literacy camps, youth pathways, AI workshops, and community programs serving children, girls, women, and first-time learners in Lasbela. Public 2025 counters report 207 students, including 122 female students, 85 male students, 10 AI workshops, and 4 winter camps.

Visit walipak.com Our work in Lasbela (field hub) Climate resilience in Lasbela Digital literacy — Balochistan
1M+ learners · 1M+ online community

Urdu AI

Pakistan's largest Urdu-language AI education platform. Making artificial intelligence accessible through WhatsApp, YouTube, Facebook, a mobile app, and a national Dost facilitator network spanning 29 districts across Pakistan, with 7,968+ participants and 248 trainings reported on the live impact dashboard.

Visit urduai.org AI education in Pakistan

Core WANG programs

Core programs delivering direct impact in communities.

Women's Enterprise

WIRE

Women's Innovation and Rural Enterprise is a women-led social enterprise operating under WANG. It links mothers, daughters, uniforms, livelihoods, and local production into a stronger case for girls' education and household resilience.

Visit wirepk.com Women & girls in technology
Girls' Leadership

ADI

ADI, also known as Rising Voices in program history, is WANG's adolescent girls and life-skills program. It creates safe spaces, school-based learning, and community support around girls' leadership, wellbeing, and participation.

See program pathways Girl Rising in Lasbela
2,200+ Girls Supported

Pakistan Scottish Scholarship Scheme

One of WANG's longest-running education programs in Lasbela. The scholarship pathway supports girls' schooling with continuity and district-rooted trust built over more than a decade.

See education impact Scholarship archive
Relief & Resilience

Las Homes and Climate Action

Flood recovery, climate leadership, resilient housing, and solar-backed community response are core public-interest programs tied directly to Lasbela and Balochistan.
