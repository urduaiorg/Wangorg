# Codex Notes

## Reccomondations :

Based on the current live `wang.org.pk` site, these are the pieces worth carrying into the rebuilt website from the existing WordPress site.

### 1. Keep the legal and institutional proof

- Keep the registration and compliance block from the current About page:
  - Balochistan Charities Act
  - Societies Act
  - Voluntary Social Welfare Act
  - BCRA registration number and NTN
- Keep the official physical address:
  - Ahmed Abad Wang, Bela, District Lasbela, Balochistan
- Keep the public contact details, but standardize them:
  - `info@wang.org.pk`
  - primary phone / WhatsApp
  - if valid, also keep the Hub sub-office from the current Contact page

Why:
This is high-trust donor and Ad Grant material. The current site has weak presentation, but the underlying institutional data is useful and should stay.

Sources:
- [About Us](https://wang.org.pk/about-us)
- [Contact Us](https://wang.org.pk/contact-us)

### 9. Do not carry these parts forward directly

- The old homepage slider headlines
- zeroed-out impact counters
- duplicate footer blocks
- generic mission-heavy copy
- outdated initiative taxonomy like ADI / Campus Kahani as top-level navigation
- thin attachment pages and media-library leftovers
- placeholder pages like `sample-page`

Why:
These weaken trust and make the site look outdated or inconsistent.

### 10. Best practical content to migrate from the existing site

If I were choosing only the highest-value carryovers from the live site, I would keep:

1. Registration details and legal proof from About
2. Full contact block, including address and validated phone/WhatsApp
3. Organizational structure summary
4. FAQ search-intent topics
5. Best 3-6 success-story videos
6. Awards / recognition posts
7. WALI historical problem framing
8. Select climate-action and girls-education legacy proof for the timeline

### Bottom line

The existing live site is still useful as a source of:

- institutional proof
- legacy program history
- award credibility
- FAQ intent
- media/video proof

It is not useful as a model for:

- navigation
- homepage structure
- copy quality
- information hierarchy
- design

Recommendation:
- Treat the old site as a source archive, not as a design template.
- Extract facts, proof, and legacy milestones from it.
- Keep the new site’s strategy, architecture, and visual system.
